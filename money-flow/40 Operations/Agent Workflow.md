Before substantial work, update your own row in [[05_Agent_Coordination|Agent Coordination]]. Do not overwrite another agent row unless there is an agreed handoff.

After work, mark your row `done` or `blocked`.

## Drift Rules

Do not create duplicate command centers or competing current-phase notes.

Do not describe future phases as implemented.

Do not let completed phases remain marked active.
