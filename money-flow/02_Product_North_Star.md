# Product North Star

Canonical product note: [[30 Strategy/Product North Star|Product North Star]]

Money Flow is a strategy-first, mandate-driven trading platform. Routing, execution, dashboards, and automation exist to make strategy decisions safer and more operable; they do not replace strategy evidence.

Current priority: preserve completed SV2.0.2 / SOR / MF-ORIG / EV-AUDIT1 evidence truth while using PT-RT1 Paper Trading as a forward-observation substrate. PT-RT remains observation-only: public mainnet strategy truth, synthetic paper ledgers, separate testnet plumbing audit rows, no production rule promotion, no live trading, and no private/signed/order endpoints from the strategy-truth lane.
