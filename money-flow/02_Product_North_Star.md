# Product North Star

Canonical product note: [[30 Strategy/Product North Star|Product North Star]]

Money Flow is a strategy-first, mandate-driven trading platform. Routing, execution, dashboards, and automation exist to make strategy decisions safer and more operable; they do not replace strategy evidence.

Current priority: preserve the completed SV2.0.2 / SOR / MF-ORIG / EV-AUDIT1 evidence truth and scope PT-RT1 only if the founder separately approves a real-time public market-data paper-observation runtime. PT-RT1 would be observation-only with no exchange orders, no private/signed endpoints, no API keys, and no live trading.
